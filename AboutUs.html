Protecting Young Children in the Digital Age

What we Offer

At the Digital Compass Association we’re here to help parents, caregivers, and educators navigate digital experiences for young children. We use research-backed approaches, provide practical tools, and connect the community through use of shared experiences and insights.

Our Mission

We Advocate for the proper and purposeful use of screen time in young children. Our goal is to ensure that digital engagement enhances, rather than hinders, a child’s development and well-being.
